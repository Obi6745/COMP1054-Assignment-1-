Good Days

SZA

About the Song

Good Days" was released as a single by SZA on December 25, 2020. The song explores themes of optimism, personal growth, and the hope for better days ahead, despite past struggles and uncertainties. It resonates deeply with listeners who are navigating their own journey of self-discovery and healing.
